# Discord Checkpoint 2025

Looking ahead to 2025, what can we expect from Discord Wrapped and Checkpoints?

## Future Features

As Discord continues to grow, users are hoping for a native, in-app Wrapped experience similar to Spotify.
-   **Instant Stats:** No need to request data packages.
-   **Shareable Stories:** Vertical video format summaries.
-   **Server Leaderboards:** See who was the most active in your server.

## Preparing for 2025

To ensure you have the best data for your 2025 checkpoint:
-   **Stay Active:** Engage in your favorite servers.
-   **Enable Data:** Make sure you haven't disabled data collection if you want personalized stats.

2025 promises to be another big year for Discord communities!
