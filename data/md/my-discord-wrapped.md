# My Discord Wrapped

"My Discord Wrapped" is a personal summary of your activity on the platform.

## Personal Stats

When you generate your wrapped, you get insights into:
-   **Voice Chat Hours:** How long you spent talking.
-   **Messages Sent:** The total volume of your text chat.
-   **Top Servers:** Which communities you were most active in.
-   **DMs vs Servers:** A breakdown of where your time went.

## Sharing

Most tools allow you to generate an image card of "My Discord Wrapped" that is perfect for sharing on social media or with your friends on Discord itself.
